What is Phi Kappa Psi?
Here at UCI, the Cal Kappa chapter of Phi Kappa Psi is one of the oldest fraternities on campus. Founded on campus is 1991, we have a growing legacy of brotherhood excellence, and we stand out from our peers. With an impressive network of alumni, over 80,000 living nationwide, joining our chapter means more than joining another campus organization. Members of Phi Psi work diligently to increase their scholarship, expand their social circle, and engage in meaningful philanthropy. Phi Psi brothers have set the highest standards on campus, and will continue to do so in the future.
