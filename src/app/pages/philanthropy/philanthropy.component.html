Hunter's Pledge

In the wake of Hunter Watson’s death as a passenger in a distracted driving accident, Hunter’s Phi Kappa Psi fraternity brothers at Syracuse University and the University of Alabama and friends at Pennsylvania State University held events to build awareness of the dangers of distracted driving. Proceeds from donations, sponsorships and sale of T-shirts will go to expanding the safe driving campaign to other campuses and for student scholarships.

Phi Psi 500

Every year, Phi Psi teams up with the local Santa Ana Boys & Girls Club to host Phi Psi 500 outdoors at the Anteater Recreation Center. It includes a cotton candy, face paint, bouncy houses, catered food, sports, and a full day of fun in the sun. We booth on Ring Road and the Student Center to gather donations throughout the week. Last year, we made an additional donation of over $3000 to the Boys & Girls Club.

FRESH Basic Needs

45% of UCI students report to be experiencing food insecurity and 5% report to be experiencing housing insecurity. Phi Kappa Psi volunteers year-round at the FRESH Food Pantry to help fight basic needs insecurity. We understand that meeting the basic needs of our community greatly impacts our mental and physical health, academic performance, work productivity, and overall success.

One Walk

In support of JDRF and their efforts to find a cure for type 1 diabetes, we participate in the annual One Walk in Anaheim, help setup the tracks, and donate to the diabetes research foundation. Phi Kappa Psi Cal Kappa has proudly supported JDRF and participated in the JDRF One Walk for the past 5 years.
